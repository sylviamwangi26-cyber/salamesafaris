OUR JOURNEY

A Passion for African Wildlife

Founded in 1995, Salame Safaris began with a simple dream: to share the breathtaking beauty of African wildlife with the world while supporting conservation efforts and local communities.

What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Africa's most trusted safari companies. Our commitment to sustainable tourism, authentic experiences, and exceptional service has earned us the trust of over 50,000 travelers from around the globe.

Today, we continue to lead the way in responsible wildlife tourism, partnering with conservation organizations and employing local guides who bring unparalleled knowledge and passion to every adventure.
