Help Center

Your guide to booking, safaris, and travel support

How to Book

You can book your safari through our website, WhatsApp, or email. Once confirmed, our team will send pickup details and itinerary.

Pickup & Transport

Pickup is available from hotels, airports, and agreed locations. Exact pickup time will be shared after booking.

What to Carry

  • Comfortable clothing
  • Sunscreen & hat
  • Camera & binoculars
  • Personal medication
  • Travel documents

Safari Dos & Don’ts

  • Follow guide instructions at all times
  • Do not feed wild animals
  • Respect local communities
  • Do not litter
