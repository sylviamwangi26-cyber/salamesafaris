Help Center

Everything you need to know before, during, and after your safari

How do I book a safari?

You can book directly through our booking page, WhatsApp, email, or social media. Once submitted, our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm details.

Do you offer custom safari packages?

Yes. All our safaris can be fully customized based on your travel dates, budget, interests, and group size.

Which countries do you serve?

We work with travelers from all over the world including Europe, Asia, America, Africa, and the Middle East.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and selected international payment options depending on your country.

Is Kenya safe for tourists?

Yes. Kenya is one of Africa’s top tourist destinations. We ensure your safety through trusted guides, secure transport, and reliable partners.

Can I cancel or reschedule my safari?

Yes. Our cancellation and rescheduling policies depend on the package and time before travel. Contact us for specific details.

Do you offer airport pickup?

Yes. Airport pickup and drop-off can be arranged as part of your safari experience.
